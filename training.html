Training & support

Versed Global provides flexible Salesforce and fundraising training and support, tailored to your roles, configuration and programmes. Training can be delivered remotely or on-site, depending on your needs.

Training options

Training is designed to build confidence and capability within your team, so you're not dependent on external support for day-to-day tasks.

Salesforce user training

Practical training for end users on how to navigate and use Salesforce effectively – tailored to your configuration and workflows.

Admin training

Training for Salesforce administrators on configuration, reports, dashboards and basic customisation – building internal capacity.

Fundraising training

Training on trust and foundation fundraising – including prospect research, grant writing and pipeline management.

Refresher sessions

Short, focused sessions to refresh skills or onboard new team members to existing systems and processes.

Documentation

Creation of user guides, process documentation and training materials tailored to your specific setup.

Ongoing support

Flexible support packages for organisations that need regular help with configuration, troubleshooting or advice.

Talk through your training & support needs

Share how your Salesforce org is currently used, who needs the most help and what "good" would look like in 6–12 months. From there, a realistic training and support plan can be shaped together.
