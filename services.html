Core Salesforce service areas

Every engagement is scoped around your actual needs — not a fixed package. These are the core areas we work across, often in combination.

01
Discovery & Solution Design

Structured discovery to understand your programmes, fundraising, reporting and governance requirements — translating them into a Salesforce design that supports your strategy.

Structured discovery to understand your programmes, fundraising, reporting and governance requirements. This includes mapping existing processes, clarifying user journeys and translating them into a Salesforce design that supports your strategy rather than forcing you to work around the system. Delivered through workshops, interviews and documentation reviews.

02
Build & Configuration

Configuration of Salesforce — including NPSP — to match your data model: custom objects, fields, page layouts, record types, validation rules and security settings.

Configuration of Salesforce (including Nonprofit Success Pack where appropriate) to match your data model and workflows: custom objects and fields, record types, page layouts, validation rules, security and sharing, profiles and permission sets — aligned with best practice for charities, corporates and public-sector institutions. Clean, documented builds designed for your admins to maintain.

03
Automation & Workflow

Design and build of Flows and other automation to reduce manual admin — reminders, approvals, grant cycles and stewardship workflows that keep work moving.

Design and implementation of Flows and other automation to reduce manual admin: automated tasks and reminders, email notifications, approvals, service pipelines, grant cycles and stewardship workflows that keep work moving without over-complicating the build. Every automation is documented and designed to be understandable by your internal team.

04
Data Migration & Quality

Support cleaning and migrating data from spreadsheets or legacy systems — clear templates, deduplication rules and practical guidance for long-term data hygiene.

Support with cleaning and migrating data from spreadsheets or legacy systems into Salesforce, designing imports that preserve history while improving quality. This includes clear templates, deduplication rules and practical guidance so your team can maintain good data habits long-term. Pre-migration assessment and post-migration validation included.

05
Reporting & Dashboards

Tailored reports and dashboards for leadership, fundraising teams and boards — income by source, pipeline health, service volumes and funder-specific metrics.

Tailored reports and dashboards for leadership, fundraising, service teams and boards: income by source, pipeline health, service volumes, outcomes and funder-specific metrics that matter to trustees, senior management and grant-makers. Built to be readable and maintainable by non-technical staff without external support.

06
Support & Optimisation

Light-touch ongoing support and iterative improvements — small enhancements, new automations, reporting tweaks and periodic reviews to ensure continued value.

Light-touch, ongoing support and iterative improvements so your CRM evolves with your organisation. This can include small enhancements, new automations, reporting tweaks and periodic reviews to ensure your Salesforce investment continues to deliver value. Available as a retainer or on a project-by-project basis — no need for a heavyweight support contract.

Data & Reporting

Data, reporting and ongoing improvement

Strong data management and clear reporting are the backbone of a CRM that delivers real value. We design data architectures, migration processes and dashboards that turn Salesforce data into actionable insights.

Stage 1
Assess & Extract

Audit your existing data sources — spreadsheets, legacy CRMs, manual records. Identify gaps, duplicates and quality issues before any migration begins.

Stage 2
Clean & Structure

Apply deduplication rules, validation templates and field mapping. Data is shaped into the correct Salesforce structure before a single record is imported.

Stage 3
Migrate & Validate

Staged import into Salesforce with post-migration validation checks. Every record is verified before go-live — history preserved, quality improved.

Stage 4
Report & Improve

Custom dashboards and reports built for your leadership, fundraising and board — plus ongoing reviews to keep data quality high as your organisation grows.

Approach to Salesforce projects

Versed Global works in partnership with your team — combining consultancy and hands-on build. The aim is not to drop in a complex system and leave, but to embed something your staff can understand, own and develop.

01
Collaborative Design

Workshops and regular check-ins with key users validate design decisions before major build choices are made — ensuring alignment with your fundraising and programme needs from day one.

02
Transparent Delivery

Clear documentation, configuration logs and admin-friendly naming conventions so your internal team can understand what has been built and why every decision was made — no black boxes.

03
Skills Transfer

Training and handover designed to grow your internal capacity — from super-user training through to admin-level guidance — so you are not dependent on external support for basic changes.
