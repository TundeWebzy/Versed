Get in touch

Versed Global operates as a UK‑based, remote‑first consultancy, working with charities, corporates and public‑sector organisations on Salesforce CRM and trust and foundation fundraising.

How contact typically works

Most conversations begin with a short email or message summarising your context: the type of organisation you are, the systems you use now, the fundraising or Salesforce challenges you are facing and what a good outcome would look like over the next 6–18 months.

From there, a 30‑minute discovery call can be arranged to explore your requirements, timelines and constraints in more detail. This is a practical conversation focused on whether Versed Global is the right partner, what is realistic within your capacity and budget, and which work should be tackled first.

Where there is a clear fit, you receive a scoped proposal that outlines options, deliverables and indicative costs. Where another specialist or approach would be better, this will be explained honestly and alternative routes signposted where possible.
