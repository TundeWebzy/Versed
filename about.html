About Versed Global

Versed Global is a specialist consultancy that designs, implements and supports Salesforce-based CRM systems, delivers targeted trust and foundation fundraising, and builds strategic websites that enhance visibility and drive organisational growth for charities, corporates and public-sector organisations.

How we started

Versed Global Ltd was founded to close a gap many organisations experience: the disconnect between technical systems, digital presence and the strategic goals they are meant to support. We saw too many charities and businesses with powerful tools sitting idle – not because the technology was wrong, but because nobody had taken the time to understand how people actually work before building the solution.

What drives us

We believe that every organisation doing meaningful work deserves systems that genuinely support its mission – not just technically, but humanly. Behind every CRM record is a real person. Behind every grant application is a community waiting for change. That understanding shapes everything we build, from data architecture to the words on a webpage.

How we work

This consultancy brings together deep experience in Salesforce CRM architecture, professional web design and hands-on fundraising, ensuring that clients receive practical, integrated solutions built for real-world use – not theoretical frameworks or generic implementations.

The heart behind Versed Global

Versed Global was not born in a boardroom. It grew from a deeply held conviction that technology should serve people – not the other way around.

We have seen, first-hand, what happens when a charity finally gets a system that works the way its team thinks. The relief in a fundraiser’s voice when they can pull a pipeline report in seconds instead of hours. The confidence of an administrator who no longer dreads the annual funder review because the data is clean, complete and tells the story it needs to tell.

Those moments matter to us – deeply. They are not just project milestones; they are the reason this company exists.

We also understand what it feels like to be the organisation that has been let down before: the CRM implementation that was delivered on time but never used, the website that looked polished but didn’t convert a single enquiry, the fundraising strategy that sat in a drawer gathering dust. We have sat across the table from those teams, listened to their frustration, and made a quiet promise to do things differently.

That promise is simple: we will take the time to understand before we build. We will be honest about what is achievable. And we will stay until the solution genuinely works – not just technically, but for the people who use it every day.

Why Versed Global exists

Too often, organisations invest in systems and strategies that never quite deliver on their promise. We exist to change that.

CRM systems that go unused

Organisations invest in expensive CRM systems only to find that the system does not support how teams actually work. Reports do not answer the questions funders ask. Data quality degrades. Staff stop using the system. We build CRM solutions that fit your team from day one.

Websites that fail to connect

Many organisations have websites that fail to communicate their value clearly, convert visitors effectively, or adapt to how stakeholders engage digitally. An ineffective online presence means missed opportunities for visibility, engagement and income generation.

Fundraising without infrastructure

Fundraising teams frequently operate without the systems infrastructure or strategic digital presence needed to manage complex pipelines, steward relationships properly, or demonstrate impact to funders and supporters. We bring systems and strategy together.

What we do

Versed Global offers three interconnected service areas, designed to work together or independently depending on your organisation’s needs and strategic priorities.

Salesforce CRM

Design, implementation, optimisation and ongoing support for Salesforce-based systems – configured specifically for charities, social enterprises, CSR teams and public-sector programmes. From data architecture to custom workflows and reporting dashboards.

Professional Web Design

Strategic website design and development that enhances your digital visibility, communicates your impact effectively, and converts visitors into supporters, donors or customers. Built with responsive design, accessibility, SEO best practices and clear calls to action.

Trust & Foundation Fundraising

Targeted support for trust, foundation and statutory fundraising – from strategy development and prospect research through to grant writing, application management and funder stewardship. Grounded in data and aligned with your organisational capacity.

Integrated Solutions

Where systems, digital presence and fundraising need to work as one – aligning your Salesforce configuration, website strategy and income generation so that data, reporting, communication and workflows support your real organisational goals.

Our commitment to you

We do not measure success by how many features we deliver or how quickly we close a project. We measure it by whether your team feels genuinely supported – and whether the solution makes your working life easier, not harder.

We listen first

Before a single field is configured or a line of code is written, we sit with your team and listen. We ask about frustrations, workarounds and the things that keep you up at night. The best solutions start with understanding, not assumptions.

We are honest

If something is not achievable within your budget, timeline or licence, we will tell you – clearly and kindly. We would rather have a difficult conversation early than deliver something that does not work. Trust is built through honesty, not through telling people what they want to hear.

We care about outcomes

A project is not finished when the system goes live. It is finished when your team is confident, your data is clean and your stakeholders can see the difference. We stay until that happens, because anything less would not sit right with us.

Our values

These are not words on a wall. They are the principles that shape every conversation, every decision and every line of work we deliver.

Integrity

Honest advice, even when it is not what you want to hear. Transparency on what is achievable, at what cost and in what timeframe informs how every engagement is scoped, priced and delivered. No overselling, no hidden costs.

Equity

A commitment to supporting organisations working with underserved communities and addressing systemic barriers is built into our practice. Accessibility, inclusive design and equitable service delivery inform everything we build.

Excellence

High standards in solution design, code quality, data governance, writing and delivery mean careful, sustainable solutions rather than quick fixes. Every system, website and strategy is built to last.

Collaboration

Working alongside client teams, transferring skills and building internal capacity instead of creating dependency. Your team should be able to maintain and develop solutions long after the engagement ends.

Learning

Continuous learning in technology, fundraising practice, digital strategy and policy ensures clients benefit from current best practice. The sector evolves rapidly – we stay current so you do not have to.

Impact

Every project is measured not just by technical delivery but by real organisational outcomes: increased efficiency, improved fundraising results, enhanced visibility, stronger stakeholder engagement and measurable return on investment.
