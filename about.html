About Versed Global

Versed Global exists to bridge the gap between strong fundraising and robust CRM systems. We combine deep fundraising experience with hands-on Salesforce expertise to help mission-driven organisations secure funding, manage stakeholders and make smarter decisions through better technology.

Why Versed was founded

Too many organisations have either strong fundraising but weak systems, or strong systems but no fundraising strategy.

Versed Global was created to remove these technical and fundraising bottlenecks so teams with important missions can access the resources, data and systems they need to serve people and communities effectively.

We're a remote-first UK consultancy with founder-led delivery and the flexibility to grow into a multi-consultant firm as demand increases.

What makes us different

Deep fundraising knowledge

Years of hands-on experience in trust/foundation fundraising, grant writing, pipeline management and funder relationships across UK and international funders.

Salesforce specialisation

Practical Salesforce configuration expertise tailored to charity, corporate and public-sector needs – not generic implementations but systems that genuinely support your work.

Mission alignment

We only work with mission-driven organisations making genuine social impact. No corporate sales teams or generic B2B – we understand charity governance, public-sector compliance and social enterprise challenges.

Guided by clear values

Every Versed engagement reflects these core principles that shape how we work with clients and approach every project.

How Versed works

Lean & flexible

Starting as founder-led consultancy with capacity to scale. We take on direct client projects alongside selective subcontracting with Salesforce partners and agencies.

Primary UK base with fully remote delivery model. Online discovery, builds, training and strategy sessions, with UK in-person options where helpful.

Collaborative partnerships

We work with Salesforce implementation partners, fundraising agencies, impact evaluators and technology vendors to deliver comprehensive solutions.
