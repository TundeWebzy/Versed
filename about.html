About Versed Global

Versed Global is a specialist consultancy that designs, integrates, implements and supports Salesforce‑based CRM systems and targeted trust and foundation fundraising for charities, corporates and public‑sector organisations. The focus is on helping mission‑driven, corporate and public‑sector teams manage relationships, programmes and funding more effectively.

Why Versed Global exists

Too many teams have strong fundraising but weak systems, or strong systems but no coherent fundraising approach.

Versed Global was created to remove these technical and fundraising bottlenecks so organisations with important missions can access the systems, skills and resources they need to serve people and communities effectively.

By aligning Salesforce CRM with real‑world fundraising and programme needs, Versed Global helps organisations manage relationships, programmes and funding in a more joined‑up and accountable way.

Who we are and what we do

Specialist Salesforce consultancy

Versed Global designs, integrates, implements and builds Salesforce CRM databases and related systems for charities, corporate organisations and government or public‑sector institutions.

Targeted fundraising support

Alongside technical delivery, Versed Global provides fundraising support for mission‑driven organisations, including trust and foundation strategy, pipeline creation, grant writing and review.

Closing the systems–fundraising gap

The consultancy exists to close the gap between strong income generation and strong systems by ensuring Salesforce and fundraising work together rather than in isolation.

Guided by clear values

Every Versed Global engagement is shaped by a set of values that emphasise impact, ethics, equity, quality, collaboration and learning.

Our values in practice

How decisions are made at Versed Global

  • • Does this improve impact for people and communities served?
  • • Is the approach transparent, ethical and respectful of data?
  • • Are we enabling, not replacing, client capacity?
  • • Are we learning and documenting as we go?

Impact

Integrity

Equity

How Versed Global works

Lean & flexible

Versed Global is structured to deliver high‑quality, project‑based and ongoing support from the outset, with capacity to involve experienced Salesforce and fundraising professionals as needed.

Remote‑first delivery

Operating primarily from the UK, work is delivered online – discovery, integration, builds, training and strategy – with options for in‑person workshops or implementation support for UK‑based clients.

Collaborative partnerships

Versed Global collaborates with Salesforce implementation partners, fundraising and communications agencies, impact evaluators and technology vendors to deliver joined‑up solutions.
