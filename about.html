About Versed Global

Versed Global is a specialist consultancy that designs, implements and supports Salesforce-based CRM systems and delivers targeted trust and foundation fundraising for charities, corporates and public-sector organisations.

Versed Global Ltd was founded to close a gap many organisations experience: the disconnect between technical systems and the income strategies those systems are meant to support.

This consultancy brings together deep experience in Salesforce CRM architecture and hands-on fundraising, so that clients receive practical solutions built for real-world use – not theoretical frameworks or generic implementations.

Why Versed Global exists

Too often, organisations invest in expensive CRM systems only to find that the system doesn't support how teams actually work. Reports don't answer the questions funders ask. Data quality degrades. Staff stop using the system.

At the same time, fundraising teams frequently operate without the systems infrastructure needed to manage complex pipelines, steward relationships properly or demonstrate impact to funders.

Versed Global was created to address both problems together: building CRM systems that genuinely support fundraising, and delivering fundraising that is grounded in data, governance and sustainable practice.

What we do

Versed Global offers two interconnected service areas, designed to work together or independently depending on your organisation's needs.

Salesforce CRM

Design, implementation, optimisation and ongoing support for Salesforce-based systems – configured specifically for charities, social enterprises, CSR teams and public-sector programmes.

Trust & foundation fundraising

Targeted support for trust, foundation and statutory fundraising – from strategy development and prospect research through to grant writing and funder stewardship.

Integrated solutions

Where fundraising and systems need to work as one – aligning your Salesforce configuration with your income strategy so that data, reporting and workflows support real fundraising goals.
