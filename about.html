About Versed Global

Versed Global is a specialist consultancy that designs, implements and supports Salesforce-based CRM systems, delivers targeted trust and foundation fundraising, and builds strategic websites that enhance visibility and drive organisational growth for charities, corporates and public-sector organisations.

Versed Global Ltd was founded to close a gap many organisations experience: the disconnect between technical systems, digital presence, and the strategic goals they're meant to support.

This consultancy brings together deep experience in Salesforce CRM architecture, professional web design, and hands-on fundraising, ensuring that clients receive practical, integrated solutions built for real-world use – not theoretical frameworks or generic implementations.

Whether it's a CRM system that genuinely supports your team's workflow, a website that communicates your impact and converts visitors into supporters, or a fundraising strategy grounded in data and best practice, Versed Global delivers solutions that work together strategically to support your mission.

Why Versed Global exists

Too often, organisations invest in expensive CRM systems only to find that the system doesn't support how teams actually work. Reports don't answer the questions funders ask. Data quality degrades. Staff stop using the system.

Similarly, many organisations have websites that fail to communicate their value clearly, convert visitors effectively, or adapt to how stakeholders engage digitally. An ineffective online presence means missed opportunities for visibility, engagement and income generation.

Meanwhile, fundraising teams frequently operate without the systems infrastructure or strategic digital presence needed to manage complex pipelines, steward relationships properly, or demonstrate impact to funders and supporters.

Versed Global was created to address these problems together: building CRM systems that genuinely support fundraising, designing websites that strategically enhance visibility and drive growth, and delivering fundraising that is grounded in data, governance and sustainable practice.

What we do

Versed Global offers three interconnected service areas, designed to work together or independently depending on your organisation's needs and strategic priorities.

Salesforce CRM

Design, implementation, optimisation and ongoing support for Salesforce-based systems – configured specifically for charities, social enterprises, CSR teams and public-sector programmes. From data architecture to custom workflows and reporting dashboards.

Professional Web Design

Strategic website design and development that enhances your digital visibility, communicates your impact effectively, and converts visitors into supporters, donors or customers. Built with responsive design, accessibility, SEO best practices and clear calls to action.

Trust & Foundation Fundraising

Targeted support for trust, foundation and statutory fundraising – from strategy development and prospect research through to grant writing, application management and funder stewardship. Grounded in data and aligned with your organisational capacity.

Integrated Solutions

Where systems, digital presence and fundraising need to work as one – aligning your Salesforce configuration, website strategy and income generation so that data, reporting, communication and workflows support your real organisational goals.

Our values

Versed Global operates according to a set of principles that shape how work is delivered, how client relationships are managed, and how every project is approached from initial scoping through to completion.

Integrity

Honest advice, even when it's not what you want to hear. Transparency on what is achievable, at what cost and in what timeframe informs how every engagement is scoped, priced and delivered. No overselling, no hidden costs.

Equity

A commitment to supporting organisations working with underserved communities and addressing systemic barriers is built into our practice. Accessibility, inclusive design and equitable service delivery inform everything we build.

Excellence

High standards in solution design, code quality, data governance, writing and delivery mean careful, sustainable solutions rather than quick fixes. Every system, website and strategy is built to last.

Collaboration

Working alongside client teams, transferring skills and building internal capacity instead of creating dependency. Your team should be able to maintain and develop solutions long after the engagement ends.

Learning

Continuous learning in technology, fundraising practice, digital strategy and policy ensures clients benefit from current best practice. The sector evolves rapidly – we stay current so you don't have to.

Impact

Every project is measured not just by technical delivery but by real organisational outcomes: increased efficiency, improved fundraising results, enhanced visibility, stronger stakeholder engagement and measurable return on investment.
