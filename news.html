Featured insight

What makes a CRM genuinely useful for fundraising?

Many organisations adopt Salesforce with the promise that it will transform fundraising and reporting. In practice, the difference between “another system to feed” and a genuinely useful CRM often comes down to a few design choices: how closely the database mirrors real fundraising journeys, how easy it is to surface the right information at the right time and how well people are supported to use it.

A useful CRM supports decision‑making at every level. Funders can be seen in the context of previous contact, proposals and reports. Fundraisers can see what needs attention this week, not just what happened last year. Leadership can see how secure income is over the next 6–18 months, and where to invest effort.

Versed Global focuses on implementations and refinements that reflect how teams actually work: aligning Salesforce objects and fields with programme delivery, trust and foundation pipelines, corporate partnerships and impact reporting rather than generic templates alone.
