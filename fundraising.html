Trust & foundation fundraising that delivers

Versed Global supports organisations through the full fundraising cycle – from strategy and prospecting to bids, reporting and renewals – combining fundraising practice with the systems needed to sustain it.

Targeted fundraising support

Support is focused on trust and foundation fundraising, CSR and institutional funding that fit your mission, scale and capacity. Work ranges from one‑off strategy and case for support development through to hands‑on bid writing, pipeline building and Salesforce‑aligned tracking.

The aim is to help organisations move from reactive fundraising towards a deliberate, multi‑year approach: fewer last‑minute bids, more time invested in the right funders and stronger stewardship for long‑term partners.

Alongside delivery, emphasis is placed on leaving behind tools, templates and processes so your team can maintain and grow institutional income without permanent external dependency.

Where support is focused

  • Trusts, foundations and institutional funders
  • Corporate CSR and ESG‑linked funding
  • Multi‑year programmes and core cost funding
  • Pipeline and bid tracking in Salesforce

Full fundraising cycle, end to end

Support can cover the full cycle or specific stages, depending on your needs. The flow below shows how strategy, research, bids and stewardship connect into a coherent fundraising system.

Stage 1
Strategy & needs
Clarifying your case for support, funding goals and the programmes you want to grow.
Stage 2
Prospect research
Mapping funders that fit your themes, geography and scale.
Stage 3
Pipeline design
Prioritising opportunities over 12–36 months and defining next steps.
Stage 4
Bids & applications
Developing high‑quality narratives, budgets and supporting evidence.
Stage 5
Stewardship
Reporting, relationship management and renewal planning.
Stage 6
Learning & systems
Using data from Salesforce and funder feedback to refine your approach.

Every stage is documented. You receive clear pipelines, templates and guidance, not just finished bids, so your team understands how decisions are made and what needs to happen next.

Salesforce is used as a live tool, not a filing cabinet. Stages, tasks and reporting can be configured so your pipeline and stewardship are tracked in the same place as your programmes and contacts.

What this work delivers

Increased funding success

Targeted applications, stronger alignment with funder priorities and clearer evidence typically improve success rates and reduce wasted effort on low‑fit opportunities.

A sustainable pipeline

A live, prioritised pipeline makes it easier to plan over multiple years, coordinate activity across staff and report clearly to trustees, executives and programme leads.

Internal capability

Templates, trackers, Salesforce fields and reports are designed so staff can continue building on the work rather than starting again after support ends.
