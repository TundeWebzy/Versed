What we offer

Each engagement is tailored to your organisation's capacity, context and goals — delivered standalone or as part of an integrated Salesforce and fundraising project.

01

Fundraising strategy

A clear, realistic trust and foundation fundraising strategy aligned with your mission, programmes and income targets — including pipeline planning and resource mapping.

02

Prospect research

Identification and qualification of trusts, foundations and statutory funders — prioritised by fit, capacity and likelihood of success.

03

Grant writing

Compelling, evidence-based grant applications tailored to funder priorities — from initial expressions of interest through to full applications.

04

Pipeline management

Support managing your fundraising pipeline — tracking prospects, applications and relationships in a structured way, ideally within Salesforce.

05

Funder stewardship

Building and maintaining strong funder relationships — from reporting and impact communication to renewals and cultivation.

06

Systems integration

Alignment of your fundraising workflows with Salesforce — so that prospect tracking, reporting and stewardship are supported by your CRM.
