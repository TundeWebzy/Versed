Trust & foundation fundraising

Versed Global provides targeted trust and foundation fundraising support – from strategy development and prospect research through to grant writing and funder stewardship – for charities and mission‑driven organisations. Where relevant, this work integrates with Salesforce to create joined-up systems and workflows.

What we offer

Each engagement is tailored to your organisation's capacity, context and goals. Services can be delivered as standalone support or as part of an integrated Salesforce and fundraising project.

Fundraising strategy

Development of a clear, realistic trust and foundation fundraising strategy aligned with your mission, programmes and income targets – including pipeline planning and resource mapping.

Prospect research

Identification and qualification of trusts, foundations and statutory funders aligned with your work – including prioritisation based on fit, capacity and likelihood of success.

Grant writing

Compelling, evidence-based grant applications and proposals tailored to funder priorities – from initial expressions of interest through to full applications.

Pipeline management

Support with managing your fundraising pipeline – tracking prospects, applications and relationships in a structured way, ideally within Salesforce.

Funder stewardship

Guidance on building and maintaining strong funder relationships – from reporting and impact communication to renewals and cultivation.

Systems integration

Where needed, alignment of your fundraising workflows with Salesforce – so that prospect tracking, reporting and stewardship are supported by your CRM.

Talk through your fundraising priorities

Share your current approach, capacity and targets, and explore what realistic, phased support could look like for your organisation.
