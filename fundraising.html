Trust & foundation fundraising that delivers

Versed Global provides specialist support across the full trust and foundation fundraising cycle – from strategy and pipeline development through to compelling grant applications and ongoing stewardship. We help small to mid-sized charities and social enterprises secure sustainable institutional funding.

Comprehensive fundraising support

Fundraising strategy & planning

Development of tailored trust/foundation strategies aligned with your mission, capacity and funding landscape. Includes opportunity analysis, priority setting and multi-year pipeline planning to build sustainable income streams.

Prospect research & pipeline building

Comprehensive research identifying suitable funders (trusts, foundations, CSR budgets, public bodies) matched to your work. Creation of prioritised prospect lists with tailored approaches, eligibility analysis and funding histories.

Grant applications & proposal writing

High-conversion grant writing following best practice structure and funder requirements. Compelling narratives, outcomes-focused proposals, budgets, evaluation plans and compelling cases for support that secure funding.

Full fundraising cycle support

Relationship management

Ongoing stewardship of funder relationships through reporting, updates and demonstrating impact. Preparation for meetings, site visits and renewal conversations to build long-term partnerships.

Monitoring, evaluation & reporting

Systems and processes to track funder outcomes, demonstrate impact and meet reporting requirements. Support with Salesforce reporting setup for grant tracking and funder-specific metrics.

Capacity building & training

Training for internal teams on trust fundraising best practice, proposal development, funder relationship management and pipeline tracking using Salesforce or other systems.

What clients gain

Increased funding success

Higher grant win rates through targeted applications, compelling storytelling and alignment with funder priorities and criteria.

Sustainable pipelines

Robust, prioritised prospect pipelines with clear next steps, reducing reliance on last-minute applications and building long-term funder relationships.

Internal capability

Teams equipped with templates, processes and systems (integrated with Salesforce where appropriate) to continue successful fundraising independently.

Collaborative fundraising support

Versed works alongside your team rather than replacing it. We build your internal fundraising muscle through hands-on support, clear documentation and knowledge transfer, ensuring you retain the relationships and expertise long-term.

Strategic focus

We help you focus on the highest-value opportunities rather than scattering effort across too many funders.

Practical tools

Templates, trackers and processes you can use immediately and adapt for your organisation's specific needs.

Integration with Salesforce

Fundraising pipelines and reporting configured within your CRM so activity tracking and funder stewardship become part of routine operations.
