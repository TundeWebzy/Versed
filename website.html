Frequently asked questions

Common questions about our website design and development services.

How long does it take to build a website?
+

Timeline depends on project scope and complexity. A small business site typically takes 4–6 weeks from initial discovery to launch. Larger, more complex sites with custom features and Salesforce integration typically take 8–12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the proposal phase.

Do you provide content writing services?
+

Yes. We can provide professional copywriting services to create compelling, SEO-optimised content for your website. We can also work with your existing content or collaborate with your in-house team. Content strategy and structure planning is included in all projects.

Will I be able to update the website myself?
+

Absolutely. We build websites with user-friendly content management systems (WordPress or custom admin panels) so you can easily update text, images, news and other content. We provide comprehensive training and documentation to ensure your team feels confident managing the site.

Can you integrate our website with Salesforce?
+

Yes, this is one of our core strengths. We can connect your website to Salesforce for lead capture, contact form submissions, event registrations, donation processing, member portals and more. All data flows seamlessly between your website and Salesforce CRM.

Do you offer website hosting and maintenance?
+

We can arrange secure, reliable hosting as part of your project, or work with your preferred hosting provider. We offer optional ongoing maintenance and support packages covering security updates, backups, performance monitoring and content updates.

Will my website be accessible and WCAG compliant?
+

Yes. We build all websites to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA accessibility standards as a minimum, ensuring your site is usable by people with disabilities. This includes proper semantic markup, keyboard navigation, screen reader compatibility and sufficient colour contrast.

Can you redesign our existing website?
+

Yes. We offer website redesign services, either as a complete rebuild or incremental improvements to your existing site. We can migrate content, preserve SEO rankings and ensure a smooth transition with minimal disruption to your operations.

What happens after the website launches?
+

After launch, we provide a settling-in period (typically 30 days) to address any bugs or minor adjustments. We deliver full documentation, training and handover materials. You can then manage the site independently or opt for ongoing support and maintenance packages.
