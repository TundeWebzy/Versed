Frequently asked questions

Click any question to expand.

How long does it take to build a website?

Small business sites typically take 4–6 weeks from discovery to launch. Larger sites with custom features or Salesforce integration typically take 8–12 weeks. Detailed timelines are provided at proposal stage.

Do you provide content writing services?

Yes — professional, SEO-optimised copywriting is available. We can also work with your existing content or collaborate with your in-house team. Content strategy planning is included in all projects.

Will I be able to update the website myself?

Absolutely. We build with user-friendly CMS (WordPress or custom admin panels) so you can easily update content. Comprehensive training and documentation ensure your team feels confident.

Can you integrate our website with Salesforce?

Yes — this is one of our core strengths. Lead capture, event registrations, donation processing, member portals and more can all flow seamlessly between your website and Salesforce CRM.

Do you offer hosting and maintenance?

We can arrange secure, reliable hosting or work with your preferred provider. Optional ongoing maintenance packages cover security updates, backups, performance monitoring and content updates.

Will my website be WCAG compliant?

Yes. All websites are built to WCAG 2.1 Level AA as a minimum — proper semantic markup, keyboard navigation, screen reader compatibility and sufficient colour contrast included.

Can you redesign our existing website?

Yes — complete rebuilds or incremental improvements. We can migrate content, preserve SEO rankings and ensure a smooth transition with minimal disruption to your operations.

What happens after the website launches?

A 30-day settling-in period covers any bugs or minor adjustments. Full documentation, training and handover materials are delivered. Ongoing support and maintenance packages are available.
