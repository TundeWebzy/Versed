Why choose Versed Global?

Versed Global brings Salesforce implementation, web design and fundraising strategy together so that systems, digital presence, income and governance move in a single direction rather than pulling against each other.

Step 1 · Understand
Start with how your organisation really works

Workshops, interviews and light-touch reviews uncover how fundraising, delivery and reporting operate today – where Salesforce and your website help, where they hinder and where data is being duplicated or missed.

Step 2 · Design
Shape Salesforce and websites around those workflows

Objects, fields, automations, reports and website structures are designed to mirror real processes: prospecting, award management, delivery and reporting – not just generic templates.

Step 3 · Integrate
Join up systems, digital presence and fundraising

Fundraising strategy, governance and reporting are threaded through the build, so that income plans, risk registers and funder obligations are visible inside Salesforce and communicated effectively through your website.

Step 4 · Equip
Build confidence, not dependency

Documentation, handover sessions and role-specific training help internal teams understand how the system and website work, what has been decided and how to adapt them as programmes grow.

Frequently asked questions

Click any question to expand the answer.

What is included in a Salesforce implementation?

Our implementations include needs analysis, system design, data migration, configuration, user training and ongoing support — all scoped to your requirements and budget.

Do you design websites for businesses?

Yes — for businesses, charities and public-sector organisations. From single landing pages to complete business websites, all responsive and accessible.

Can you support fundraising strategy and delivery?

Yes. We design strategies, build prospect pipelines, support bid writing and establish governance so funding plans are practical and deliverable.

Who do you typically work with?

Charities, social enterprises, businesses, corporates and public-sector teams that need Salesforce, web design and fundraising to move in the same direction.

How long does a typical Salesforce project take?

A basic implementation typically takes 8–12 weeks; more complex integrations 12–20 weeks. A detailed timeline is provided during scoping.

Do you work with the Nonprofit Success Pack (NPSP)?

Yes, we specialise in NPSP configuration — accounts, contacts, donations, relationships and all functionality tailored to nonprofit fundraising.

What support do you provide after implementation?

Comprehensive post-launch support: user training, system optimisation, troubleshooting and strategic advice — available as retainers or project-based packages.

How can fundraising strategy integrate with Salesforce?

We design your CRM to support fundraising workflows — from prospect research and pipeline management to grant tracking and reporting — so the system enables rather than hinders.

Can you help migrate data from our current system?

Yes. We handle data assessment, cleansing, mapping and migration from existing systems, validating all records before go-live.
