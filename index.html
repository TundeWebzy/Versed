What Versed Global does

Versed combines deep fundraising experience with hands-on CRM and data skills, giving clients a partner who understands both income generation and the systems that support it. We help you remove technical and fundraising bottlenecks so you can focus on impact.

Salesforce CRM design & build

End-to-end Salesforce implementation for charities, corporates and public-sector bodies – including data model design, configuration, automation, reporting and user training.

Trust & foundation fundraising

Support across the full fundraising cycle: case for support, prospect research, pipeline creation, bid writing, reporting and stewardship, drawing on best practice similar to specialist fundraising consultancies.

Ongoing optimisation & support

Continuous improvement for your CRM and fundraising programmes – from new features and dashboards to refining your grant pipeline based on learning and results.
