Frequently asked questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process and support.

What is included in a Salesforce implementation?
+

Our Salesforce implementations include comprehensive needs analysis, system design, data migration, configuration, user training and ongoing support. We tailor the scope to match your organisation's specific requirements and budget.

Do you design websites for businesses?
+

Yes, Versed Global provides professional web design services for businesses, charities and public-sector organisations. We create responsive, accessible websites that effectively communicate your services, values and impact – from single landing pages to complete business websites.

Can you support fundraising strategy and delivery?
+

Yes. We help design fundraising strategies, build realistic prospect pipelines, support bid writing and establish governance so that funding plans are practical and deliverable, not just aspirational targets.

Who do you typically work with?
+

We work with charities, social enterprises, businesses, corporates and public-sector teams that need Salesforce, web design and fundraising support to move in the same direction, rather than working in silos.

How long does a typical Salesforce project take?
+

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A basic implementation typically takes 8–12 weeks, while more complex integrations may take 12–20 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the scoping phase.

Do you work with the Nonprofit Success Pack (NPSP)?
+

Yes, we specialise in NPSP configuration for charities and nonprofits. We have extensive experience with accounts, contacts, donations, relationships and all NPSP functionality tailored to nonprofit fundraising requirements.

What support do you provide after implementation?
+

We offer comprehensive post-launch support including user training, system optimisation, troubleshooting, and strategic advice. Support packages can be customised as ongoing retainers or project-based.

How can fundraising strategy integrate with Salesforce?
+

We design your CRM to support your fundraising workflows, from prospect research and pipeline management to grant tracking and reporting. This alignment ensures your system enables, not hinders, your fundraising programme.

Can you help migrate data from our current system?
+

Yes. We handle data assessment, cleansing, mapping and migration from existing systems. We ensure data integrity throughout the process and validate all migrated records before go-live.
